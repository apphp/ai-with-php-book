# Applications of Agents in AI

• Autonomous Systems: Drones, robots, self-driving cars.

• Personal Assistants: Siri, Alexa, and similar intelligent assistants.

• Gaming: NPCs (non-playable characters) and AI agents in gaming environments.

• Smart Grids and Energy Management: Use of agents in optimizing energy consumption.

• Healthcare: AI agents assisting in diagnostics and personalized treatment recommendations.

• E-commerce: Intelligent shopping assistants and recommendation systems.
